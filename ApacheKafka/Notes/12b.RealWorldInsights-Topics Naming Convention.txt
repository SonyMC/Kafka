Topics Naming Convention
------------------------
- Refer : https://cnr.sh/essays/how-paint-bike-shed-kafka-topic-naming-conventions
- Naming a topic is " free for all"
- It's better to enforce guidelines in your cluster to ease mgmt
- you are free to come up with your own guidelines
_ Recommended convention:
	- <message type>.<dataset name>.<data name>.<data format>
